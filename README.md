This project is an integrated **E-commerce, POS, and Business Management System**
designed to help businesses manage online sales, in-store transactions, inventory,
customer activities, and reporting from one centralized dashboard. The platform
supports three main user roles—**Admin, Customer, and Cashier**—each with role-based 
access to system functions. Customers can browse products, manage carts, and place orders
online; cashiers can process in-store sales, receive payments, and issue receipts through
the POS interface; while administrators manage products, monitor stock levels, oversee users, 
and generate business reports. By combining online shopping features with point-of-sale
operations, the system ensures smooth synchronization between sales, payments, and inventory management.

The system is built with a modular architecture using **Django** 
as the backend framework, with apps for accounts, products, carts,
orders, POS, inventory, and reports. It automates critical business workflows
such as reducing stock after online or in-store sales, recording payments,
tracking order history, and generating sales and profit reports. 
Development is structured in phases, beginning with authentication and product management,
followed by shopping cart and order processing, then POS operations,
and finally inventory analytics and reporting. 
This phased approach allows the system to grow from a minimum viable product
into a complete smart business solution, with future enhancements such as sales prediction, 
customer analytics, and recommendation systems.
